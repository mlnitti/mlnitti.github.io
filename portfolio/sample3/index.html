Venue Navigator Logo

The fourth and most recent assignment was the logo/wordmark creation. I created a fictional app called Venue Navigator. I used the V and the N as the letters for my logo. I positioned the bottom of the V to fit between the right-hand valley in the N. I then put dotted lines between the letters to indicate a path. I used green because most of the people using the app would be at sports stadiums so it represents grass. The idea of the app is to allow people at sports stadiums, concert venues and convention centers to find their way around a venue. The app would map out exits, bathrooms, sections, vendors and so on. It would then give the best and most efficient path. The purpose is to save people time from aimlessly walking around a stadium not knowing where to go.
