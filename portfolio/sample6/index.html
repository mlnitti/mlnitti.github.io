Pecha Kucha

Nitti pecha kucha - iteration - cdm (1) (1) from MichaelNitti3

My Pecha Kucha was something I was very proud of. I thought I made it engaging and interesting enought to keep the attention of the class. I tried to make it with relatable examples of iteration that anyone can understand. I also advanced my slides in their design as I went through them to signify that my presentation also went through some process of iteration. It also sticks out in my mind because it was the most unique presentation I have ever given and I was originally skeptical on how it would play out.
