The Dark Knight: Genre Remix

The second assignment was the genre remix. I chose to remix one of The Dark Knight posters, specifically the one with Heath Ledger as Joker on it. The Dark Knight is considered an action/adventure superhero film. The first thing I thought of when I decided to do The Dark Knight was the famous painting by Van Gogh, Starry Night. So I cropped Joker out of the original poster and put him on a copy of the painting and called it “A Starry Knight.” This new genre I would consider to be something like a documentary on Van Gogh, played by Heath Ledger.
